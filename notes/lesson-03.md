# Lesson 03 — Using Generative AI Responsibly

## Core Concepts

* Generative AI systems can produce harmful, biased or incorrect outputs
* Responsible AI focuses on fairness, safety, transparency and accountability
* Hallucinations occur when an LLM generates false or misleading information
* AI systems must be evaluated continuously, not trusted blindly
* Responsible AI is both a technical and business responsibility

## Important Takeaways

* AI outputs should always be reviewed in critical business scenarios
* Human oversight is essential when using LLMs in production
* RAG and grounding techniques help reduce hallucinations
* Responsible AI is not only about ethics, but also reliability and trust
* Good AI systems require safety layers, filtering and clear UX boundaries

## Potential Risks

* Hallucinations and false information
* Harmful or unsafe outputs
* Bias and unfair treatment
* Privacy and security risks
* Prompt injection and misuse

<img width="859" height="410" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/0f6d7f53-91bd-4b4a-894a-a6944a26a4e5" />


## Mitigation Strategies

* Use trusted data sources
* Add safety filters and validation layers
* Limit model behavior through system prompts
* Use RAG to ground answers in company data
* Evaluate outputs regularly with testing and monitoring

## Real-World Applications

* Safe AI-powered customer support
* Internal enterprise AI assistants
* AI document search with trusted sources
* Responsible legal and insurance AI workflows
* AI copilots with human approval processes

## Ideas / Thoughts

* Build AI systems with validation and review steps
* Combine RAG with trusted company documents
* Explore AI content filtering and moderation systems
* Design workflows where humans remain in control

## Questions

### Why is Responsible AI important?

Because AI systems can confidently generate incorrect, biased or harmful outputs that affect users and business trust.

### Why is human oversight still necessary?

LLMs are probabilistic systems and cannot guarantee correctness or factual accuracy.

### How can hallucinations be reduced?

Using RAG, grounding techniques, system prompts, validation workflows and trusted data sources can significantly reduce hallucinations.
